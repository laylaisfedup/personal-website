Bookshelf

The goal for this part of my website is to have a list of my current library. One of my life goals is having a home where the library is the heart of it. I love collecting books across many topics — I think it keeps one humble. I'm also a huge fan of Umberto Eco's concept of the antilibrary: a collection of unread books one keeps or collects. In that spirit, the list won't be divided into neat categories. I find that boring. Instead, I'll highlight the books that made a strong impression on me, those I'd recommend to others.
